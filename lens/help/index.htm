Introduction to phpLens

PhpLens is a fast and powerful way to design, publish and maintain your databases on the Web and Intranet. PhpLens has dynamic editing that works in your Web browser, and also a set of Lenses (special data filters) such as powerLens, lookupLens and colorLens to reconfigure the appearance of your database tables in style, color and looks.
